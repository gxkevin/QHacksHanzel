How to Use the App

1) Enter the app
2) Press Start

4) Go have fun!
5) View the logs or just click view on the main menu to see where you been!

Trace Your Night

Hanzel is an application for the iPhone and iPad that lets you backtrack where you been during the night while under the influence.

  • Tells you how long you been at one location
  • Tracks where you are by pinning your location if you stay for more than a minute
  • Secured and your journey will be erase within 48 hours
